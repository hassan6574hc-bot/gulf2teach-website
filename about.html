Gulf2Teach is a specialist teacher recruitment service permanently based in the region — with real relationships and genuine knowledge of how Gulf schools hire.

Our story

A recruiter who lives
where you're going.

Most teacher recruitment agencies operate from UK offices with limited understanding of what educators walk into when they arrive in Saudi Arabia. Gulf2Teach is different.

Founded by a QTS-qualified teacher residing in Saudi Arabia and operating from the UK, Gulf2Teach was built from a simple observation: hundreds of qualified teachers wanted to teach in the Gulf, but didn't know the right people.

We exist to be that connection — making deliberate, vetted introductions between the right teacher and the right school, with regional knowledge no desk-based agency can replicate.

01

Regional knowledge

Our founder resides in Saudi Arabia with strong links across the Gulf's international school sector. All business is conducted from the UK, giving us both regional insight and full legal clarity.

02

Vetted candidates only

Every teacher in our pool has been individually assessed. Schools receive quality-controlled profiles — not bulk CVs sent to every inbox.

03

Transparent model

Schools pay a placement fee after the teacher starts. No cost to teachers, no hidden charges. Simple and clearly stated from the beginning.
